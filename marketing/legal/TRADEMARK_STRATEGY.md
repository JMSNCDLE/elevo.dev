## Priority Filing Order

### Step 1 — UK Intellectual Property Office (URGENT)
- **URL:** https://www.gov.uk/search-for-trademark
- **Classes:** 35 (Business management; Marketing services) + 42 (SaaS; AI software)
- **Cost:** ~£200 per class (£400 total for 2 classes)
- **Timeline:** 4–6 months to registration
- **Why first:** UK is first-to-file. Earliest filing date = strongest rights. Gets you ® in UK.
- **Action:** Use ELEVO's UK solicitor or file direct at https://www.gov.uk/apply-for-trademark

### Step 2 — European Union Intellectual Property Office (Within 6 months of UK)
- **URL:** https://euipo.europa.eu/eSearch/
- **Classes:** 35 + 42
- **Cost:** ~€850 for 1 class + €50 per additional class = ~€900 for 2 classes
- **Timeline:** 5–7 months to registration
- **Why:** One EUTM covers all 27 EU member states. Priority claim from UK filing date.
- **Priority claim:** File within 6 months of UK filing to claim UK filing date as priority.

### Step 3 — USPTO (After US launch)
- **URL:** https://tmsearch.uspto.gov/
- **Classes:** 35 + 42
- **Cost:** $250–350 per class online ($500–700 total)
- **Timeline:** 8–12 months to registration
- **Why:** US is use-in-commerce based — you need actual US users. File after US customer #1.
- **Note:** File "intent-to-use" application to reserve the mark before launch.

### Step 4 — Australia / Canada (Year 2)
- **AU IP Australia:** https://search.ipaustralia.gov.au/trademarks
- **CA CIPO:** https://www.ic.gc.ca/app/opic-cipo/trdmrks/srch/home
- **Cost:** ~AU$250 / CA$350 per class
- **Timeline:** 12–18 months each

## Response to Infringement

### Step 1: Gather Evidence
Screenshot everything. Note dates. Build a case file.

### Step 2: Cease & Desist Letter
Send a polite but firm C&D letter. Most cases resolve here.

### Step 3: Platform Takedown
- Social: Use platform trademark infringement report forms
- Domain: Use UDRP arbitration (WIPO) — costs ~$1,500 but fast
- App stores: Apple/Google trademark infringement report

### Step 4: Legal Action (Last Resort)
Engage a UK IP solicitor. Costs typically £5,000–20,000 for litigation.
